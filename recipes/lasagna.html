Lasagna Recipe

Photo by Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 ounce) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup of water
